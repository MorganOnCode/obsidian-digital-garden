# Page 1: Define, Prevent, Repair

Make three lists with 10–20 entries each. 

1. **Define**
	1. What are the worst things could happen?
	2. What's the vision/scenario you're most afraid of? 
	3. How is this fear affecting you?
2. **Prevent**
	1. How could I prevent each from happening?
		1. What's within your control? 
	2. What do you currently do to prevent/avoid bad things happening? 
3. **Repair**
	1.  If the worst happens, how can I fix it?
	2. How could you get back to exactly where you are now? 

# Page 2: Benefits of action

Make a list of the possible benefits if successful or partially successful. 

# Page 3: Cost of inaction

Make three lists of the costs of your inaction. In other words, if I avoid doing this thing what might I miss out on?

• 6 months
• 1 year
• 3 years

# Page 4: DO NOW

Define 1 (one!) thing you could do now, just to start building momentum on the journey.

Do it now, or schedule it.
